Cloud Storage

The term "cloud storage" is a very basic function that just serves one purpose, letting you access data from anywhere in the world. The project basically came about when i felt the need for a easy system to upload my files to later access them on another device, and thats how PiCloud was created.

View details »

Want to try out PiCloud? It'll blow your mind.

Click on register in the meny to apply for an account. Since the system is running on a Raspberry Pi the system can get overloaded quickly. You will get an email when you registration has been noted and you account created.

How do i use PiCloud? See for yourself.

After your account has been registerd you login selecting the login tag in the meny. After logging in you will be presented with the upload window that lets you add a file to your folder. Then venture on to the download section once you want to retrive your file.
